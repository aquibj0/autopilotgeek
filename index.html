Frequently Asked Questions

1. Why do I need a UI/UX Design Agency?

There won’t be any other person happier than you when you see your brand growing when you see people talking about it, you are closing multiple deals more often and we as a Design Agency have years of experience in how to make this happen.

Once you collaborate with us, we take all the responsibilities to present your vision in the most appealing and engaging way.

2. What is your design process?

We follow a five steps process that looks like this,

Step 1:  Get the requirement and content

Step 2:  Ideation and create moodboard

Step 3:  Low-Fidelity Wireframing Design and get your approval

Step 4:  High-Fidelity design and get your approval

Step 5:  Prototype all the wireframes (If required)

Step 6:  Get the project delivered

3. What is your estimated timeline for delivering a UI/UX Design project?

Mostly it depends on the project and its complexity, on average, it takes 2-3 Days to complete single page website design

4. Which design tools do you use?

Our team is experienced with modern design tools, Figma, Canva, Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Illustrator, Framer, and Indesign.

5. Do you offer enterprise UI/UX Design services?

Yes, we offer enterprise design services, just submit the request and we will get in touch with you and talk about it in detail.

6. How many years of experience in this domain?

We are Upwork’s top-rated design agency, we have been working for the last 5 years and have delivered 40,000+ hours of high-quality work.

7. What if I don't like the design?

Since our design process will have your approval on every step, you will not require to have a second thoughts on the final design.

But, in case you will have any updates regarding the contents of the final design, we will do it for free.

8. How is your pricing work?

It depends on your requirements and what kind of design you are looking for. We work in hourly and project-based pricing, you can submit the request to know more in the detail about our pricing.
